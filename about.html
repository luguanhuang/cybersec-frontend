About Cyber Report Tool

The Cyber Report tool is built by the cybersecurity team at SEIRIM and offered to the public to help raise awareness and insight into common security issues organizations need to be aware of. A more powerful version is made available for use by clients and available at seirim.com. The premium version goes deeper into news scanning for threats regarding supply chain, third party, SaaS and ancillary threats to the organization, and scans for them automatically on a scheduled basis.
